Facilities & Services

Meticulously structured features and assistance designed for your maximum comfort.

Hotel Amenities

Designed for a Carefree Island Stay

From essential in-room conveniences to local logistics, we have every detail covered for your Port Blair stay.

Multi-Cuisine Restaurant

Savor delicious Indian, Chinese, and local continental dishes served fresh at our on-site dining space.

Free High-Speed WiFi

Uninterrupted wireless internet connectivity available across all rooms and public spaces for our guests.

Intercom System

Direct communication dial-lines between all rooms and the 24-hour reception counter for rapid assistance.

24-Hour Water Supply

Continuous running hot and cold water supply provided in all attached bathroom facilities throughout your stay.

Dedicated In-room Geysers

Independent water-heating geysers installed inside every bathroom layout for individual guest control.

On-Call Laundry Service

Convenient, fast turnaround laundry and dry-cleaning services available upon request through front desk.

Two-Wheeler Rental

Rent well-maintained scooters and motorbikes on-site for custom sightseeing around Port Blair.

Airport Pickup & Drop

Hassle-free chargeable airport pick-up and drop transfers in private vehicles. Call reservations to arrange.

Custom Package Tours

Full local tour organizing, including boat ticket bookings for Ross Island, North Bay, and Cellular Jail guides.

Providing comfort, warmth, and reliable island hospitality in Port Blair. A home away from home in the Andamans.
