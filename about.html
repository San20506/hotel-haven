About Hotel Haven

A peaceful, welcoming home away from home in Sri Vijaya Puram (Port Blair), Andaman.

Our Story & Ethos

Genuine Island Hospitality

Hotel Haven was established with a singular mission: to provide comfortable, dependable, and warm accommodations for travelers exploring the beautiful Andaman and Nicobar Islands. We combine budget-friendly rates with clean, fully furnished AC rooms and personalized attention.

Situated in the peaceful Dolly Gunj area on Govt. Polytechnic Road, we offer guests a serene retreat away from the busy city streets, while keeping you exceptionally close to Veer Savarkar International Airport (only 2.5 km away) and the central ferry jetty (Haddo Harbour) for excursions to Havelock (Swaraj Dweep) and Neil (Shaheed Dweep) islands.

Our travel desk is managed by local experts who take the stress out of your holiday planning, from scheduling Cellular Jail entry tickets and arranging boat trips to booking two-wheeler rentals for self-guided island exploration.
