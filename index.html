Welcome to Hotel Haven

Comfort & Hospitality
in Dolly Gunj

Located on Govt. Polytechnic Road, Dolly Gunj, Sri Vijaya Puram, Hotel Haven is a family-run hotel offering clean, comfortable AC rooms at honest prices. We're 2.5 km from the airport, 4 km from the harbour and Cellular Jail, and 3.5 km from the main city — making us an ideal base for exploring Sri Vijaya Puram and the Andaman Islands.

Whether you're here for business or leisure, our in-house restaurant, free Wi-Fi, intercom, 24-hour water supply, and two-wheeler rentals ensure a hassle-free stay. We also arrange airport pickups, package tours, and ferry bookings.

About Hotel Haven

Your Home in Sri Vijaya Puram

Hotel Haven is a family-owned hotel on Govt. Polytechnic Road, Dolly Gunj, offering 35 well-maintained AC rooms across 5 categories. Whether you're a solo traveller, a couple, or a family of four, we have a room that fits your needs and budget.

We believe in straightforward hospitality — clean rooms, reliable service, good food, and helpful staff who know the islands well. From two-wheeler rentals to package tours, we help you make the most of your Andaman visit.

FAQ

Common Questions

What are your meal plans? +

We offer three meal plans: CP (Breakfast included), MAP (Breakfast & Dinner), and AP (All Meals Included). Prices vary by room type — check our room details for exact rates.

Do you offer airport pickup? +

Yes, airport pickup and drop is available on request (chargeable). We also offer two-wheeler rentals and can arrange package tours around the islands.

What facilities are available? +

We have an in-house restaurant serving local cuisine, free Wi-Fi, intercom in all rooms, 24-hour water supply, geysers, laundry on call, two-wheeler rentals, and package tour assistance.
