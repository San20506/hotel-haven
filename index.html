Our Philosophy

The Haven
Experience

Hotel Haven is designed to feel like an extension of the island's natural architecture. Built using locally sourced elements and structured around the coastal elevation, the property offers a curated ecosystem where luxury feels organic and rest feels inevitable.

Whether waking up to the ocean breeze or retiring after an island expedition, every touchpoint has been refined to emphasize spatial harmony, physical comfort, and an authentic connection to local hospitality.

Meet the Founders

Grounded in Local Care

We are a family-owned retreat built on the simple belief that hospitality should feel like coming home. Guided by island hospitality, our team ensures that your experience is enriched by personalized attention, local insights, and absolute comfort.

From custom island trip planning to hand-prepared local delicacies, we are here to say a warm "hi" and make sure your journey is unforgettable.

FAQ

Common Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times? +

Standard check-in is at 12:00 PM, and check-out is at 10:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out is subject to availability and prior confirmation.

Do you assist with ferry and flight transfers? +

Yes, we offer complete airport shuttle services and assist in booking high-speed catamaran ferry tickets to Havelock and Neil Islands.

Is internet connection reliable? +

We provide high-speed fiber-optic Wi-Fi in all rooms and public lounges, ensuring seamless connectivity for remote work.
